2. What Data May Be Stored

2.1 Local Data (on your device)

Cat Chaos Crash stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Gameplay progress (best run score, total fish coins collected)
  • Unlocked cat skins and currently selected skin
  • Settings (sound, music, vibration, notifications)
  • Daily login streak and challenge progress
  • Lucky wheel spin history

This data is stored using Android SharedPreferences and never leaves your device unless you use Google Play Games cloud save.

2.2 Google Play Games Data (optional)

If you sign in to Google Play Games, the following data may be stored in your Google account:

  • Saved Games (Snapshots): A backup of your gameplay progress
  • Leaderboard entries: Your best run score and total coins for ranking
  • Achievements: Unlocked achievements

This data is controlled by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

2.3 Advertising Data

Cat Chaos Crash uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect:

  • Advertising ID (resettable identifier)
  • Device information for ad personalization
  • Ad interaction data

This data is processed by Google under Google's Advertising Privacy Policy. You can control ad personalization in your device settings.

2.4 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns. This may include:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (session duration, screens viewed)
  • Crash reports (device model, app version, crash logs)
  • General demographic data (country, language)

This data is anonymized and aggregated. It cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. How to Delete Your Data

3.1 Delete Local App Data

Choose one of these methods:

Option A: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Cat Chaos Crash will permanently delete all local data stored by the app on your device.

Option B: Clear App Storage

Go to Android Settings → Apps → Cat Chaos Crash → Storage → Clear Storage (or "Clear Data")

3.2 Delete Google Play Games Data

If you used Google Play Games features, your saved games, leaderboard entries, and achievements are stored in your Google account. To delete this data:

Step 1: Open Play Games App

Open the Google Play Games app on your Android device.

Step 2: Access Settings

Tap your profile icon → Settings → Delete Play Games account & data

Step 3: Delete Specific Game Data

Select Cat Chaos Crash and confirm deletion, or use Google's Data & Privacy Dashboard to manage your data.

Note: Google controls Play Games data. For questions about Google Play Games data deletion, please refer to Google Play Games Help.

3.3 Reset Advertising ID

To reset or opt out of personalized advertising:

Go to Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID

3.4 Firebase Analytics Opt-Out

Analytics data is collected anonymously. You can limit data collection by:

  • Disabling "Usage & diagnostics" in your Android settings
  • Uninstalling the app (stops all future data collection)

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR (for EU residents) and CCPA (for California residents), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Non-discrimination: Exercise your rights without discrimination (CCPA)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the email address above.

6. Data Retention

Local app data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. We do not retain copies of your local data on our servers.

Google Play Games data is retained according to Google's data retention policies until you request deletion through Google.

Analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymized form and cannot be attributed to individual users.

7. Children's Privacy

Cat Chaos Crash is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.
