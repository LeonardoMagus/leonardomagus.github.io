Privacy Policy - Cat Chaos Crash

Last updated: January 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Cat Chaos Crash ("the App") is a free mobile game. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect personal data. However, third-party services integrated into the App may collect data:

  • Google AdMob: Advertising ID, device info, IP address for serving ads
  • Google Play Games: Game progress, achievements, leaderboard scores (if signed in)
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Google may use cookies and advertising IDs to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us for requests.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the Google Play Store listing.
