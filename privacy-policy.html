2. Summary

Cat Chaos Crash is a game. You can use it without creating an account with us.

We do not ask for your name, address, phone number, or payment details. We do not operate our own servers to store your data. The app uses third-party services (Google Play Games, Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics) that may process data under their own responsibility.

3. What Data Is Processed

3.1 Local Game Data (on your device)

The App stores the following data locally on your device to provide game functionality:

  • Gameplay progress (best run score, total fish coins collected)
  • Unlocked cat skins and currently selected skin
  • User preferences (sound, music, vibration, notifications)
  • Daily login streak and challenge progress
  • Lucky wheel spin history

Legal basis (GDPR): Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) - necessary to provide the game service.

This data is stored using Android SharedPreferences and never leaves your device unless you enable Google Play Games cloud save.

3.2 Google Play Games Services (optional)

If you sign in to Google Play Games, the following data may be processed:

Data Type Purpose
Player ID & profile Identify you for leaderboards
Saved Games (Snapshots) Cloud backup of your progress
Leaderboard entries Display rankings
Achievements Track unlocked achievements

Legal basis (GDPR): Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) - you choose to sign in.

Google is the data controller for Play Games data. See Google's Privacy Policy.

3.3 Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads using Google AdMob. Google and its partners may process:

  • Advertising ID (resettable device identifier)
  • IP address and derived approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App interaction and ad performance data

Legal basis (GDPR): Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) for personalized ads in EEA; Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) for non-personalized ads.

You can control ad personalization in your Android settings or through the consent dialog shown when you first open the app. See Google's Advertising Privacy Policy.

3.4 Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve the game. This may include:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (session duration, screens viewed)
  • Crash reports (device model, app version, error logs)
  • General demographic data (country, language)

Legal basis (GDPR): Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) - to improve app quality and fix bugs.

This data is anonymized and aggregated. It cannot be used to identify you personally. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

GDPR Rights (EEA residents)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing)
  • Lodge complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

CCPA Rights (California residents)

  • Know: Know what personal information is collected
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

To exercise your rights, see our Data Deletion Request page or contact us at leonardo.mara.33@gmail.com.
